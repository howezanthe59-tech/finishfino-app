Last Updated: February 15, 2026

FinishFino Cleaning Pros respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website or booking our services, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to deliver and improve our services:

  • Contact details: name, email, phone number, service address.
  • Booking details: service type, property type, bedrooms/bathrooms, preferred dates and times, special instructions, add-ons.
  • Payment details: deposit and balance amounts; payments are processed via third-party providers, and we do not store full card data.
  • Usage data: device/browser info, IP address, and pages visited to keep our site reliable and secure.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Schedule, confirm, and manage your cleaning bookings.
  • Process required deposits and balances.
  • Communicate confirmations, reminders, and service updates.
  • Improve our services, site performance, and customer support.
  • Prevent fraud, enforce terms, and comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Payment processors to collect required deposits and balances.
  • Service staff/contractors to perform scheduled cleanings.
  • Vendors providing scheduling, email/SMS, analytics, or security tools.
  • Authorities when required by law or to protect our rights and users.
